About me

I was born in September of 1999 in Germantown, TN. I grew up there and had an attachment to technology at a very young age. I would tinker around with just about everything computer or video game related. I then started growing a passion for technology right around the age of 12, and decided to pursue a career in the technology industry. I then sought out a degree at The University of Memphis but dropped out in 2018. I decided that I wanted to figure out what specific field I wanted to go into since the market was vast. After 3 years, I finally picked my degree back up in 2022 and decided to finish what I started in Memphis. Here I am, after those 3 years working in the IT industry, and I know exactly what I want to do; Cybersecurity.
